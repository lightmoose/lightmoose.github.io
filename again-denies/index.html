Nadine Lustre Again Denies Resurfaced Pregnancy Rumors

admin | November 4, 2019 | Uncategorized | No Comments

MANILA, Philippines – Nadine Lustre, world famous actress and singer, recently has denied reports that she is pregnant over rumors that surfaced after she posted her last Instagram photo which happened to show what seemed like a baby bump on her stomach.

For the press conference she recently had for her upcoming show, Your Moment, on October 21, Lustre has denied all of these rumors and came out to say that she has never made an announcement like this in the first place.

“Parang ako naman, if hindi naman nanggaling sa akin, sana huwag nang paniwalaan, ‘di ba (In my view, if it did not come fromme, no one should believe it, right)?” she said.

The photo was posted by one of her official fan clubs on Instagram of her at the airport. The rumor started with the commenters saying that they had noted a small bump on her tummy that seemed bigger than usual, fueling pregnancy speculations. It was not the first time Nadine was rumored to be pregnant.

There were the same rumors back in 2018 that she was pregnant in which her and her boyfriend James Reid made fun of on one of their social media posts which has now been deleted.

Aside from the recent rumors that have been circulating For the very first time in her career, Lustre will be asked to be a part of a judging panel in Your Moment, which is a talent show along with Boy Abunda and Billy Crawford. This show will debut on November 9, and the contestants will be selected by the judges for a variety of showcase in their skills.

 At the press conference, Nadine said that it took her a while to get used to judging since it involves an emotional meter, where they based their scores on the contestant’s performance.

“Honestly, napakahirap magjudge kasi lahat ng contestants magaling…. So at the end of the performance – kasi ako very emotional ako, and sabi mo nga since it’s an emotional meter – parang after every performance, ‘pag nakakatanggalan, parang ako, ‘Oh my god, sana, sana hindi sila yung natanggal,'” she said.

Nadine claims that it will be very hard to judge the contestants because they are all so good, and at the end of the performance she knows that she will be very emotional. The thought that someone will have to be eliminated after every round is something she will not be able to get used to.

“Pero siyempre meron at meron darating na mas magaling sa kanila. So talagang survival of the fittest siya talaga (But of course, there will always be someone who is better. So it’s really a survival of the fittest),” she added.

Your Moment is a two-in-one talent show looking for groups of singers and dancers. The show is a collaboration of ABS-CBN and Dutch powerhouse creator Fritz Productions, a production company that has done unscripted formats.

Aside from Filipino talent, the show will feature performers from other parts of the world.
